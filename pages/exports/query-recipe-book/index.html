    Date format recently changed

    The date format recently changed. You are no longer required or allowed to specify a time as part of start_date and end_date. Instead, we use the timezone associated with your app. Visit the Account Settings to see your timezone.

    Example change: instead of sending 2017-11-29T00:00:00-08:00, send 2017-11-29.

    You can find your Branch Key and Branch Secret on the Account Settings page of the Branch Dashboard. Give the curls below a try from any terminal!
